ILL PAIRS

Like I mentioned above in the glossary, Ill pairs are kanji that look as similar as their meanings, which makes them twice as hard to tell apart! Having them here all in one spot doesn't really help you learn anything but it's a telling reminder of just how pernicious and devious Japanese is.
