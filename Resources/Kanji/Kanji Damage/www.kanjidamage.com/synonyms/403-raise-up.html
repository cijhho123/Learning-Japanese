   raise up

上げる(あげる) To literally raise (your hands, and wave them from side to side; the roof)   $$$
 
挙げる(あげる) To metaphorically bring up something (an objection, a point) to give an example of, to cite or mention something.
 
揚げる(あげる) Hoist a flag/ deep fat fry
