査 inspect

木 (tree) + (Abe Lincoln's Hat)
★★☆☆☆
9 strokes
BETA

Onyomi

SA

Mnemonic

The tree inspects Abe's hat, to see what it SAW on the night of the assassination (the hat was eventually cleared of all wrongdoing)

Jukugo

検査(けんさ) する

checkup ★★★☆☆
検 (investigate) + 査 (inspect) = 検査 (checkup)

Medical checkup. Also, investigation, inspection, review: (fiancial audit, smog check, check the condition of goods that roll off the assemby line, etc). Has the nuance of a test where something passes or fails.
調査(ちょうさ) する

investigate ★★★☆☆
調 (check out) + 査 (inspect) = 調査 (investigate)

an investigation. Emphasizes that there are no correct answers, and implies that the investigators are talking to someone, asking questions directly. (usually used about polls, surveys, and detectives questioning suspects and/or witnesses)
捜査(そうさ)

criminal investigation ★★☆☆☆ NP
捜 (search) + 査 (inspect) = 捜査 (criminal investigation)

を 査定(さてい) する

assess ☆☆☆☆☆
査 (inspect) + 定 (plan) = 査定 (assess)
