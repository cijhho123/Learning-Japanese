電気製品(でんきせいひん)

consumer electronics ★★★☆☆
電気 (electricity) + 製 (mass production) + 品 (products) = 電気製品 (consumer electronics)

consumer electronics- anything from stereos to calculators to ipods. Radio Shack, Bic P Kan, and Yodobashi Camera are 電気製品 stores.
XXX 製(せい)

made from/in ★★★☆☆ SUF

This is a SUFFIX which has two meanings, to wit:

1：made from XXX. For instance:

木製：もくせい： made from wood. (***)

2： made in such-and-such a country.

日本製： にほんせい： things (usually electronics or clothes) made in Japan. (**)

製造：せいぞう： manufacturing (**)
製造(せいぞう)

manufacturing ★★☆☆☆ NP
製 (mass production) + 造 (produce) = 製造 (manufacturing)

manufacturing, production
