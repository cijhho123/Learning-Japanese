瞬 twinkle / tiny bit of time

目 (eye) + (nest) + (George Michael's Moustache) + (date)
★★★☆☆
18 strokes

Onyomi

SHUN

Mnemonic

You look with your eye for a tiny bit of time and decide not to date the guy with the nest embedded in his moustache - in fact, you decide to SHUN him.

Kunyomi

( が ) またた＊く to blink, but also to flicker or twinkle。 Usually used as a adjective like, "またたく ほし" (twinkling star)
★☆☆☆☆
まばた＊き ( する ) a wink,a blink 1/2 KANA
☆☆☆☆☆

Jukugo

瞬間(しゅんかん) に or の

an instant ★★☆☆☆
瞬 (twinkle / tiny bit of time) + 間 (a period of time) = 瞬間 (an instant)

an instant, the blink of an eye.
