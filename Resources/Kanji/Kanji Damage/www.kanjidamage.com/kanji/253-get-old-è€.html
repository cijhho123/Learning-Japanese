老 get old

(dizzy) + (heel)
★★☆☆☆
6 strokes

Onyomi

ROU

A bunch of old people in a ROW, (waiting for the Social Security office to open. )

Mnemonic

The old man was so senile he got dizzy while walking and fell on his heel.

Kunyomi

( に　・　を ) ふ*けてる to get old, a somewhat perjorative term: Danny Glover saying 'I'm too old for this shit!' - "Dumbledore's getting old, he makes mistakes."Also means "To be a kid but look older than you are."
☆☆☆☆☆
( が ) お＊いる another word for 'to get old, to age', but without the perjorative connotation of ふける。
★☆☆☆☆

Jukugo

老人(ろうじん)

old person.Not rude. ★★★☆☆
老 (get old) + 人 (person) = 老人 (old person.Not rude.)
