大統領(だいとうりょう)

President ★★★☆☆
大 (big) + 統 (tradition) + 領 (territory) = 大統領 (President)

受領書(じゅりょうしょ)

reciept ★★☆☆☆
受 (receive) + 領 (territory) + 書 (write) = 受領書 (reciept)

領域(りょういき)

domain ★★☆☆☆ NP
領 (territory) + 域 (region) = 領域 (domain)

a territory or domain controlled by someone. Also used in neologisms like "domain name!"
