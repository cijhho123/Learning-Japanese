誘 invite / entice

言 (say) + 秀 (excel)
★★★☆☆
14 strokes
This has both a good meaning and a bad meaning! Invite someone to a nice dinner or lure them to their doom.

Onyomi

YUU

Mnemonic

YOU excel at saying things which entice suckers. (FYI you are a televangelist, pimp, or both)

Kunyomi

( に　or を ) さ*そう invite (a friend to dinner) (or sometimes, invite an emotion, as in, a tear-jerker of a movie)
★★★★☆

Jukugo

誘惑(ゆうわく) する

tempt ★★★☆☆
誘 (invite / entice) + 惑 (be misguided or tempted) = 誘惑 (tempt )

誘導(ゆうどう) する

lead ★★☆☆☆
誘 (invite / entice) + 導 (lead) = 誘導 (lead)

to lead or conduct : A teacher leads the class on the field trip. The policewoman conducts traffic. The nuance is, 誘導 means literally going together with the people to the place.
