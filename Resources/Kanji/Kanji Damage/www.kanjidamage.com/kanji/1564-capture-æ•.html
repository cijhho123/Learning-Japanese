Kunyomi

★★★★☆
( を ) とら*える to catch THE MEANING of something - almost always figurative, in contrast to the more literal, physical つかまる。
★★☆☆☆
( が ) つか＊まる to be caught (can both be used to describe an animal being caught by a predator OR someone grabbing someone else by the arm,a crook caught in the act, etc.)
