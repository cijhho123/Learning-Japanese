居 live

(corpse) + 古 (old)
★★★★☆
8 strokes
COCK
COCK: As a stand-alone verb, when you'd normally use kunyomi, 居る is almost always written with hiragana: いる. And in a jukugo, when you'd normally use onyomi, 居る ususally uses kunyomi. It's enough to make you hate living . . .except you can't even write "I hate life!" in Japanese anymore.

Onyomi

Mnemonic

An old man living upstairs recently died and I found his corpse.

Kunyomi

い*る to be alive - to exist (only used about animals, not plants) 1/2 KANA
★★★★☆

Jukugo

Japanese-style bar ★★★☆☆ KUNKUN
