Onyomi

HEKI

Mnemonic

He's got a bad habit of stinking so bad people think he's sick.
That's a HECK of a bad habit!

Kunyomi

くせ ( になる ) bad habit. But not that bad. Gambling or hoe-hopping is like a "akushumi". 癖 is just like biting your nails or saying "like" a lot, although it is usually more unique and distinctive.
★★★☆☆

Jukugo

ｘｘｘの 癖(くせ) に YYY!!

xxx but still fuckin' yyy!! ☆☆☆☆☆ 1/2 KANA

a very disapproving way of saying "but still". As in, "He don't even have money for rent but still he just got a ＄２00 Bathing Ape shoe.": 家賃の金もないの癖に二万円bape靴を買っちゃった！
