夕 evening

★★★☆☆
3 strokes
BETAPKILL PAIRCOCK

Onyomi

none

COCK: it is the katakana "ta" character ! Unfortunately, it's pronounced "YUU." How's that for a contradiction?

Mnemonic

Anyway. When the Japanese come home in the evening after work they say TA-daima! to YOUUU.
