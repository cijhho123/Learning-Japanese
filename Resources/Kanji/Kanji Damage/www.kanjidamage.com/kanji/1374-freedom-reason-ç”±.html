由 freedom / reason

申 (humbly say/god radical)
★★★★★
5 strokes
JERKBETAPNPK

Onyomi

YUU

YOU want to be free, don't you?

Mnemonic

Looks like the God radical, but the vertical line only goes UP. That's because humans always reach UP, searching for freedom.

Jukugo

自由(じゆう) な

freedom ★★★★★
自 (my own) + 由 (freedom / reason) = 自由 (freedom)

理由(りゆう)

reason ★★★★★
理 (reason) + 由 (freedom / reason) = 理由 (reason)

reason (for doing something)
