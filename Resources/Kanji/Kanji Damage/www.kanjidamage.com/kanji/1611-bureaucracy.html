bureaucracy

PK
. . . because it is used in the kanji for bureaucrat, but mostly because it looks like a capital "B."

HOW TO TELL THEM APART: Unlike 呂, has a straight spine running down the left side- because bureaucrats are inflexible. Also because the letter "B" has a straight line on the left.
