頂 summit/climax

丁 (nail) Same-ON + (headright)
★★☆☆☆
11 strokes

Onyomi

CHOU

Mnemonic

At the climax of the movie, Margaret CHOU got a nail through her head!

Kunyomi

いただ＊き the summit of a mountain. A sort of literary or formal word. F1/2 KANA
☆☆☆☆☆
いただ＊く ：to do, have, eat, etc. used by inferior person when addressing superior person. F1/2 KANA
☆☆☆☆☆

Jukugo

頂点(ちょうてん)

peak ★☆☆☆☆
頂 (summit/climax) + 点 (point) = 頂点 (peak)

climax or peak (the zenith of his popularity / lonely at the top / peak of fascism's power/ the climax of the song, etc)
