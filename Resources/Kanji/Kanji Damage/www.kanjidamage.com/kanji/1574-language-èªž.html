主語(しゅご)

subject ★★★☆☆
主 (master/mainly) + 語 (language) = 主語 (subject)

the subject of the sentence. AKA the thing Japanese people never say.
物語(ものがたり)

tale ★★☆☆☆ KUNKUNNOKURI
物 (animal / thing) + 語 (language) = 物語 (tale)

a tale- often used in the title of a book or movie. As in 'the tale of genji' 'tale of two cities.'
