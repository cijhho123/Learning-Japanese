句 verse of a poem

(wrap) + 口 (mouth/small box radical) Same-ON
★★★☆☆
5 strokes
BETA
Don't confuse 句(verse) with 可!

可 is straight lines, because it's the nail radical - nails are straight!

But, 句 uses the 'wrapping' radical, (not hard iron, but soft paper or cloth), so it's round and organic.

Onyomi

KU

This is a sensitive love poem is about a COOCHIE.

Mnemonic

When you mouth a verse, you wrap your words in poetry.

Jukugo

文句(もんく)

complain ★★★☆☆ FP
文 (culture/ sentence) + 句 (verse of a poem) = 文句 (complain)

禁句(きんく)

forbidden, tabboo word ★★☆☆☆
禁 (prohibition) + 句 (verse of a poem) = 禁句 (forbidden, tabboo word)

俳句(はいく)

haiku ★☆☆☆☆
俳 (actor) + 句 (verse of a poem) = 俳句 (haiku)

yo mama so fat /

she went to the zoo one time /

kids fed her peanuts
