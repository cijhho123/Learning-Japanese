I'm not in the bosoZOKU gang but I'm affiliated with them, so I can act like a big shot.

Mnemonic

The bug crawling across the corpse's Moustache appears to be the genus of drosophila melogaster, the common housefly. Soon it will lay eggs in the tender hairs.
