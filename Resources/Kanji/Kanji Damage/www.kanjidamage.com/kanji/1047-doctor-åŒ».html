医師(いし)

doctor ★☆☆☆☆ NP
医 (doctor) + 師 (master, teacher) = 医師 (doctor)

a more formal word for doctor -(you wouldn't say "I need to see the 医師!" - you'd say, "I need to see the 医者（いしゃ）！”）
XXX 医(い)

a SUFFIX meaning, 'XXX branch of medicine -' in other words, 'XXXology'.
医療(いりょう)

medical treatment ★☆☆☆☆
医 (doctor) + 療 (medical therapy) = 医療 (medical treatment)

medical treatment- the kind one gets in a hospital (x-rays, MRI, surgery, etc.)
