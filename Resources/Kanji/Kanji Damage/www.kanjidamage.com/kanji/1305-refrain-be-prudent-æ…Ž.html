慎 refrain / be prudent

(leftheart) Same-ON + 真 (really)
★★★☆☆
13 strokes
FRPN
FUCKED ROOTS ALERT -- whoever invented this kanji thought that it's prudent to refrain from speaking your heart.

Onyomi

SHIN

Mnemonic

People who refrain from speaking their heart really really SHINE in Japanese society.

Kunyomi

つつし*む to be cool-headed, prudent, or discreet when it comes to something potentially disruptive. Could also be translated as 'refrain', like, 'refrain from smoking or talking loudly.' 'Don't say fuck. Watch your lanauge when you talk to nuns.'
★★☆☆☆
