Jukugo

失礼(しつれい) な

rude ★★★★☆
失 (miss out on) + 礼 (polite) = 失礼 (rude)

rude, without manners.
お礼(おれい) に

in thanks for ★★★☆☆

in thanks for. As in, "In thanks for the sweater, I bought him a moray eel."
礼儀(れいぎ)

Formal manners ★★☆☆☆
礼 (polite) + 儀 (politeness) = 礼儀 (Formal manners)

Manners used in formal situations: correct bowing proceedures, sonkeigo, respecting heirarchy.
礼金(れいきん)

key money ★★☆☆☆
礼 (polite) + 金 (gold) = 礼金 (key money)

literally 'politeness money.' - sometimes the phrase "key money" is also used - as if that explains anything. This is the legalized bribe you pay for the privilege of paying rent on a new apartment.
