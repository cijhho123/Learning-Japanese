Onyomi

Mnemonic

The YAK-thief had the misfortune to leave a fingerprint on the cliff. (and was thusly convicted of Grand Theft Precipice and sent upstate for 2-5 years)

Jukugo

厄介(やっかい) な

hard to deal with ★★☆☆☆ 1/2 KANA
厄 (misfortune) + 介 (introduce / intervene) = 厄介 (hard to deal with)

troublesome, worrying. Hard to deal with! A hard-to-fix appliance. A stubborn, persnicketty person.
