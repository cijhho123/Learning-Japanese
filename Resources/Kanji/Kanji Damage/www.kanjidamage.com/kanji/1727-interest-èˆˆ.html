興 interest

(FFFFFoolio) + 同 (same) + (animal legs)
★★★★☆
16 strokes
BETA

Onyomi

KYOU, KOU

TODAY (今日) my COLON is really interesting!

Mnemonic

Let's call that weird thing on the right side BIZARRO Foolio, because it's the same as regular Foolio but a mirror image.

Thus, we can say:

I'm interested in Foolio, but at the same time interested in BIZARRO Foolio.

Jukugo

興味(きょうみ) のある　・　がある

interest ★★★★★
興 (interest) + 味 (flavor) = 興味 (interest)

interest in something

興味のあるｘｘｘ : an intriguing xxx

きょうみがある : I'm interested in it!

興味無し！ : I'm not interested!

即興(そっきょう) の

to improvise! ★☆☆☆☆
即 (immediately) + 興 (interest) = 即興 (to improvise!)
