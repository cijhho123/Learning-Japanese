凶 terrible

メ (katakana 'me') + (pit)
☆☆☆☆☆
4 strokes
BETA
This kanji is usually only seen by itself in fortune-telling. It's like the worst fortune or whatever, so it's frequently the punchline of jokes.

Onyomi

KYOU

Mnemonic

In May, I fell into a terrible pit and I am still trapped there until TODAY (今日, pronounced KYOU)!!

Heinous! ☆☆☆☆☆

atrocious! Heinous! Some Rape of Nanking kind of shit.
