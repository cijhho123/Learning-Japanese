倹約(けんやく) する

frugal ★☆☆☆☆
倹 (thrifty) + 約 (promise/ roughly speaking) = 倹約 (frugal)

frugal, saving money, getting bargains. (BOOBOO - 倹約 is used in a good way. If you want to say someone is cheap, say 'kechi!')
