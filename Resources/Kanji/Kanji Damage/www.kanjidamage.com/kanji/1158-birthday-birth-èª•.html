Jukugo

誕生日(たんじょうび) の日

birthday ★★★★☆ FP
誕生 (birth) + 日 (sun, day) = 誕生日 (birthday)

birthday

If you want to say Happy Birthday, you'd just say 良い誕生日！　（よいたんじょうび！）.

However, if you're asking someone what day is their birthday, you'd add 'の日’　like this:
誕生日の日はイツ？　（たんじょうびのひはいつ？）
