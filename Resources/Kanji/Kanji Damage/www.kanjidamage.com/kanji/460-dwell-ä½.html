住 dwell

(personleft) + 主 (master/mainly)
★★★★★
7 strokes

Onyomi

JUU

JEWISH people dwell all over the earth, because they're having themselves a Diaspora.

Mnemonic

You're a person who dwells in the master's house, that's why you have to pay rent!

Kunyomi

( xxxに ) す＊む to dwell in xxx.
★★★☆☆

Jukugo

住所(じゅうしょ)

address ★★★☆☆
住 (dwell) + 所 (area / attribute) = 住所 (address)

住民(じゅうみん)

residents ★☆☆☆☆
住 (dwell) + 民 (folk) = 住民 (residents)

原住民(げんじゅうみん)

native ☆☆☆☆☆
原 (original/ high plain) + 住民 (residents) = 原住民 (native)

a person or animal native to the area. Indigenous.
