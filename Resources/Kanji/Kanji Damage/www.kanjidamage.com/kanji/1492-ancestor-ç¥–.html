Jukugo

先祖(せんぞ)

one's family ancestors ★★☆☆☆
先 (earlier/ the tip) + 祖 (ancestor) = 先祖 (one's family ancestors)

one's own personal ancestors. Ancestor worship.
祖先(そせん)

far-away ancestor ☆☆☆☆☆
祖 (ancestor) + 先 (earlier/ the tip) = 祖先 (far-away ancestor)

includes the meaning of 先祖, but it's more broad. 祖先 can also refer to ancestors dating back to monkeys, fish, germs, etc. "The two words/phyla of fish/languages share a common ancestor."
