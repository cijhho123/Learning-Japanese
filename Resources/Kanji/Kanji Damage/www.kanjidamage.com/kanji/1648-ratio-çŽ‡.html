率 ratio

(beret) + (Eazy) + (Sparkles) + 十 (ten)
★★★☆☆
11 strokes
BETA

RITSU

Mnemonic

Eazy-E tries to keep a ratio of one beret to ten sparkles. Any more sparkles and it might get TOO FABULOUS.
