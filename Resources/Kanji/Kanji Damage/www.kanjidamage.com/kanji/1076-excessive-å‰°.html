過剰(かじょう) XXX

excessive XXX ★☆☆☆☆ PRE
過 (surpass / too much) + 剰 (excessive) = 過剰 (excessive XXX)

PREFIX (sometimes SUFFIX!) meaning excessive - sometimes as in an overdose of drugs, an excess of personnel, but usually an excess of an attitude or behavior. This prefix is the only time you'll ever see the 剰　kanji.
自信過剰(じしんかじょう)

overconfidence. ☆☆☆☆☆
自信 (self-confidence) + 過剰 (excessive XXX) = 自信過剰 (overconfidence.)
