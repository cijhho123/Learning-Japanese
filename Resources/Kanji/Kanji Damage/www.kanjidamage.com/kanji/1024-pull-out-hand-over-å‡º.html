出 pull out / hand over

(pit) + 一 (one/line radical)
★★★★★
5 strokes
PN

Onyomi

SHUTSU

He pulls out his revolver and SHOOTS.

Mnemonic

出 is two pits + a vertical line (which we'll think of as a string or cable)

This looks like you're hauling something up; Pulling the line of string out of the pits.

Kunyomi

( を ) だ＊す I take it out
★★★★★
( が ) で＊る it comes out
★★★★★

Jukugo

出口(でぐち)

exit. ★★★★★ KUNKUN
出 (pull out / hand over) + 口 (mouth) = 出口 (exit.)

は or が 出来る(できる)

possible ★★★★★ 1/2 KANA
出 (pull out / hand over) + 来 (come/future) = 出来る (possible)

it's possible to do.(I can run, I can shit, but I can't run and shit at the same time.)
へ 出発(しゅっぱつ)

departure ★★★★☆ NP
出 (pull out / hand over) + 発 (launch) = 出発 (departure)

departure of train, bus, plane.
xxxの 出身(しゅっしん)

a thing from XXX ★★★★☆ SUF
出 (pull out / hand over) + 身 ((one's own) flesh) = 出身 (a thing from XXX)

a thing made in XXX, a person from XXX。
引き出し(ひきだし)

drawer ★★☆☆☆
引 (pull) + 出 (pull out / hand over) = 引き出し (drawer)

drawer or chest of drawers.
