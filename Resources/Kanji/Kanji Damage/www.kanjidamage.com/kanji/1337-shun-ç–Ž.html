疎 shun

マ (Mama) + 止 (stop) + 束 (bundle of sticks)
★☆☆☆☆
12 strokes

Onyomi

SO

Everyone shuns Some One.

Mnemonic

I wish your mama would stop shunning bundles of sticks. She's a homophobe.

Kunyomi

( に ) うと*い emotionally distant from, or: to not know a lot about
★☆☆☆☆

Jukugo

疎外感(そがいかん)

alienation (usually it means alienation from society or the world at large - more of a withdrawl or angsty thing) ☆☆☆☆☆
疎 (shun) + 外 (outside) + 感 (feeling) = 疎外感 (alienation (usually it means alienation from society or the world at large - more of a withdrawl or angsty thing))

疎遠(そえん) になる

become estranged from ☆☆☆☆☆
疎 (shun) + 遠 (far) = 疎遠 (become estranged from)

to become estranged from, alienated from (usually used about a specific person or people: ex-husband, family, group of friends, etc.)
