探 look for

(handleft) + (nice thick pie) + 木 (tree)
★★★★☆
11 strokes

Onyomi

TAN

Mnemonic

Use your hands to climb the tree and look for the TAN thick pie which is stuck in the top branch (long story).

Kunyomi

さが*す to search for something you want but don't have (a good restaraunt, a store with bargains, sanity).
★★★★☆

Jukugo

探偵(たんてい)

private detective ★★★☆☆
探 (look for) + 偵 (detective) = 探偵 (private detective)

BOOBOO: Most books say this means 'detective' but really it means private detective, not police. (police detective is 刑事　－けいじ）. There's a huge P.I. industry here - they have advertisements in all the magazines and even posters on walls on the street!
