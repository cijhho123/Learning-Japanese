生 life / birth

主 (master/mainly) + (rifle)
★★★★★
5 strokes
DUPEPNSTRONG

Onyomi

SEI, SHOU

If you can SAY something, it SHOWS you're alive.

Mnemonic

The master gave you life, and the master can take your life away with the rifle

Kunyomi

( が or に ) う＊まれる to be born. Like, 1999年に生まれた (born in 1999)
★★★☆☆
( を ) う＊む To give birth. Can be used figuratively as in, "these conditions breed terrorists," or "He gave birth to the film industry," or " produce profits."
★★★☆☆
( を ) い＊きる to live.
★★★☆☆
( を ) い＊かす usually means, "make the most of (a resource or a spot of good luck)" or　"to use wisely. (a scarce resource)" . . . but but it also can mean a doctor literally bringing a patient back to live i.e. after cardiac arrest.
★☆☆☆☆
( が ) は＊える sprout (hairs as well as plants)
★☆☆☆☆

Jukugo

先生(せんせい)

teacher ★★★★★
先 (earlier/ the tip) + 生 (life / birth) = 先生 (teacher)

生活(せいかつ)

lifestyle ★★★★★
生 (life / birth) + 活 (vivid, lively) = 生活 (lifestyle)

学生(がくせい)

student ★★★★★
学 (knowledge) + 生 (life / birth) = 学生 (student)

生(なま) XXX

live and direct. ★★★☆☆ PRE

this is hard. It got tons of meanings: nama tv is live, not prerecorded. Nama beer is poured straight from the bottle, not like a fancy mixed drink. Nama information is first hand. Nama meat is raw.
人生(じんせい)

life (in general) ★★★☆☆
人 (person) + 生 (life / birth) = 人生 (life (in general))

Life, as in someone's entire life, or life in general:. "He lived a quiet life." "Life is a bitch." "Life span."
生き物(いきもの)

living thing ★★☆☆☆
生 (life / birth) + 物 (animal / thing) = 生き物 (living thing)

living, moving thing . . . (BOOBOO：it doesn't apply to plants! Only little dudes that can move around and forage for food are いきもの)
