告 inform

(hurl) + 口 (mouth/small box radical)
★★★☆☆
7 strokes

Onyomi

KOKU

Mnemonic

Kunyomi

つ*げる to tell
★★☆☆☆

Jukugo

と or を 告白(こくはく) する

confess ★★★☆☆
告 (inform) + 白 (white) = 告白 (confess)

to confess- both to a crime and to having a huge crush on me.
広告(こうこく)

advertisement ★★★☆☆
広 (wide) + 告 (inform) = 広告 (advertisement)

an advertisement - although nowadays people use the Japlish word シーエム (CM, short for 'commercial.')
報告(ほうこく)

report ★★☆☆☆ NP
報 (data) + 告 (inform) = 報告 (report)

A report - or maybe just 'data' is better?
Compared to the related term 情報 (news report), 報告 has more of a nuance of a scientific, governmental, or corporate feeling.
Think about it like this: 報告 are commissioned by private agencies, and then journalists do a 情報 about the 報告.
忠告(ちゅうこく) する

admonish ★☆☆☆☆
忠 (loyalty) + 告 (inform) = 忠告 (admonish)

admonishing negative advice: "Don't open that door!" "Don't swim naked!"
