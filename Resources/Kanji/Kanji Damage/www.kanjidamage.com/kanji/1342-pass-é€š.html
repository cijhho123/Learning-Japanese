通 pass

(motion) + (pass radical)
★★★★☆
10 strokes

Onyomi

TSUU

Mnemonic

TWO cars move and pass through a tunnel.

Kunyomi

( が ) とお＊る to go past???
★★★★☆
( を ) とお＊す I make something go in or through - either literally (i.e. threading a needle, escorting a guest inside) or figuratively (to force one's opinion through a meeting or to keep crying all night)
★★★★☆
( で or に ) かよ＊う go to (used only with places like school or work, where you go there regularly)
★★★☆☆
どお＊り a big road, an avenue. (NUBI - 通りis a SUFFIX which goes after the name of the avenue. You'd never say, "Hey, which way is the 通り?") NUBI
★★★★☆

Jukugo

普通(ふつう) の or　に

normal / normally ★★★★★
普 (normal) + 通 (pass) = 普通 (normal / normally)

交通(こうつう)

traffic ★★★★☆
交 (combine) + 通 (pass) = 交通 (traffic)
