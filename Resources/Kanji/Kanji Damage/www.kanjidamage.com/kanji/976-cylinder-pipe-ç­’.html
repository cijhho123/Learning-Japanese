筒 cylinder /pipe

竹 (bamboo) + 同 (same)
★☆☆☆☆
12 strokes
ILL PAIR

Onyomi

TOU

Mnemonic

He keeps using that same bamboo cylinder to suck his TOES.

Kunyomi

つつ usually defined as pipe but more accurately, it means cylinder.

Chimney, exhaust pipe, drainage pipe, gun barrel, canister, are all 筒. （BOOBOO: unlike the related kanji 管　(tube), which can bend, 筒 is always straight).
★★★☆☆

Jukugo

封筒(ふうとう)

envelope ★☆☆☆☆
封 (seal in) + 筒 (cylinder /pipe) = 封筒 (envelope)
