許可(きょか)

permission ★★★☆☆
許 (allow) + 可 (possible) = 許可 (permission)

免許(めんきょ)

license ★★☆☆☆
免 (exemption / license) + 許 (allow) = 免許 (license)

an exemption from something, or a license to do something. (i.e. drivers' license: 運転免許証 (うんてん　めんきょ　しょう）
