素敵(すてき)

Wonderful! ★★★☆☆ 1/2 KANA
素 (element) + 敵 (enemy) = 素敵 (Wonderful!)

Wonderful! Magnificent! This is usually used only by women, and it is used in reference to things made or done by a third party - you wouldn't say your friend's cooking was すてき, but you'd say a dress your friend wore was すてき.
