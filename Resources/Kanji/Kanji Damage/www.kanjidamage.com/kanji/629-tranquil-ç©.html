穏 tranquil

(wheat) + (nest) + (snout) + (bottomheart)
★☆☆☆☆
16 strokes
SOLO

Onyomi

ON - you don't need this

it's tranquil ON the surface but deep within the twisted mazes of your mind, you're as psycho as I am.

Mnemonic

The bird wished with all her heart to build a tranquil nest out of wheat, to shield her family from the snouts of predators.

Kunyomi

おだ*やか ( な ) calm or mild - used about personality AND peaceful places, calm weather, mild ocean. A person who doesn't get mad. 1/2 KANA
