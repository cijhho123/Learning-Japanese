寝 go to bed

(crown) + (guy so macho he has two dicks) + (snout) + (George Michael's Moustache) + 又 (again/crotch radical)
★★★★★
13 strokes

Onyomi

SHIN - you don't need this.

When I went to bed, I saw a SHINY jewel in my dream.

Mnemonic

I wanted to sleep with this guy in spite of his snout and tasteless crown, because I heard he has two dicks in his crotch.

Kunyomi

ね*る lie down, go to bed (BOOBOO： it does NOT mean 'sleep,' which is 眠る!)
★★★★★
ね＊かす put someone to bed (a baby, a senile aunt, etc)
★★★☆☆

Jukugo

昼寝(ひるね) する

a nap ★★★☆☆ KUNKUN
昼 (noon) + 寝 (go to bed) = 昼寝 (a nap)

寝不足(ねぶそく)

lack of sleep ★★☆☆☆ KUNKUN
寝 (go to bed) + 不足 (shortage of) = 寝不足 (lack of sleep )

寝坊(ねぼう) する

sleep late, oversleep ★☆☆☆☆ KUN ON
寝 (go to bed) + 坊 (boy / monk) = 寝坊 (sleep late, oversleep)

寝室(しんしつ)

bedroom ★☆☆☆☆
寝 (go to bed) + 室 (room suffix) = 寝室 (bedroom)
