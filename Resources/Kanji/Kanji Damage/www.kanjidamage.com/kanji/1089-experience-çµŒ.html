経 experience

糸 (string) + (take a dump) Same-ON
★★★★☆
11 strokes

Onyomi

KEI

Mnemonic

Everyone I know has had the experience of taking a dump and string came out. It's OK.

Kunyomi

( が ) た*つ usually used in the phrase : 時間が経つ・ time passes. Usually a long time. Like, "After three days, the letter arrived."
★★★☆☆

experience ★★★★☆

the economy (経済学（けいざいがく）= economics) ★★★☆☆

経験者(けいけんしゃ)

survivor ★★☆☆☆
経験 (experience) + 者 (professional) = 経験者 (survivor)

someone with experience - usually a negative experience. In which case the closest English word would be 'survivor' (of abuse, discrimination ,etc.)
