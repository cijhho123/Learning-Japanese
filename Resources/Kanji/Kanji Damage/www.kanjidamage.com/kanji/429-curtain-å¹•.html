幕 curtain

(opium) Same-ON + 市 (small city/dagger　radical)
★☆☆☆☆
13 strokes
literal curtains, as well as metaphorical curtains, as in 'The mafia who are controlling the city do it in secret, behind the black curtains (黒幕).'

Onyomi

MAKU

Old MACK Donald had a farm, and on this farm he had beef curtains.

Mnemonic

There's an opium addict hiding behind the curtain holding a dagger. When you walk by, he'll jump out and stab you, take your shit and sell it for dope.

Kunyomi
