取 take

耳 (ear) + 又 (again/crotch radical)
★★★★★
8 strokes
DUPE

Onyomi

SHU. You won't use this.

Mnemonic

The pervert cameraman only takes close-ups of ears and crotches, especially when he can See Her Underpants.

Kunyomi

と*る To take a picture. Also, to pick something up off a table or shelf, pick up a small thing like glasses or a plate. Also means to get something (good marks on a test, a prize, etc,).
★★★☆☆

Jukugo

取り替える(とりかえる)

to exchange ★★☆☆☆
取 (take) + 替 (substitute) = 取り替える (to exchange)

取り消す(とりけす)

to cancel an order, to withdraw. ★☆☆☆☆
取 (take) + 消 (erase) = 取り消す (to cancel an order, to withdraw. )

取り扱い(とりあつかい)

management ★☆☆☆☆
取 (take) + 扱 (treatment) = 取り扱い (management)

Treatment - but unlike 扱う, 取り扱う is ONLY used for objects or cases. i.e. The police are handling the racial hate-crime very carefully.
取り締まり(とりしまり)

crackdown ★☆☆☆☆
取 (take) + 締 (tighten) = 取り締まり (crackdown)

a crackdown, a regulation of something that was unregulated
