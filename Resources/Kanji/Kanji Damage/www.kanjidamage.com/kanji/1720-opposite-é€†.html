逆 opposite

(motion) + 屯 (bent dagger)
★★★★☆
9 strokes

Onyomi

GYAKU

If you tell me I GOTTA COOL down, I'll do the opposite.

Mnemonic

You'll note that the bent dagger radical is bent to the left, rather than the right. Because this kanji means opposite!

So now we can say . . .

Whichever way you move,I'll move the opposite !
