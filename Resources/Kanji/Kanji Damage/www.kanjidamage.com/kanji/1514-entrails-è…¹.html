腹 entrails

月 (moon/organ) + (dork!)
★★★☆☆
13 strokes
FPILL PAIRCOCK

Onyomi

Mnemonic

Only a dork would eat organs and entrails.

Kunyomi

はら or おなか entrails!
The kunyomi of his one is tricky because if it's written like this:　腹, it's pronounced はら、 but if it's written with the honorary 'お', like this: お腹、 then it's pronounced おなか。 Thus, this kanji is a COCK.
★★★☆☆

切腹(せっぷく) する

seppuku ★☆☆☆☆ F
切 (cut / important) + 腹 (entrails) = 切腹 (seppuku)

when a samurai commits suicide by slicing his guts out. (切腹 is kind of formal - in casual conversation, you'd say 腹きり (はらきり） instead。)
