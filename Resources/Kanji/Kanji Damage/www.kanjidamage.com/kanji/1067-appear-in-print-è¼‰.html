載 appear in print

十 (ten) + (spear) + 車 (car)
★★☆☆☆
13 strokes

Onyomi

SAI

The celebrity SIGHed, upon learning that his latest drug-arrest was bout to appear in print .

Mnemonic

He jumped in his car with ten spears to drive to the magazine office and stop them from printing it.

Kunyomi

( を or に ) の＊せる to include something in the final version, include in the published report. Confusingly, 載せる can also can mean 'put on top of something' : 'put the skis on vehicle' or 'put something on top of the cabinet'
★★★☆☆
( に ) の＊る to be printed or reported
★★☆☆☆

Jukugo

掲載(けいさい)

to be reported. ★★☆☆☆

Basically means the same thing as 載せる、 but 掲載 is a little more formal: to be reported.
xxxを 搭載(とうさい) してる

come equipped with ☆☆☆☆☆
搭 (board) + 載 (appear in print) = 搭載 (come equipped with)

to come equipped with XXX (built-in camera, etc.)
