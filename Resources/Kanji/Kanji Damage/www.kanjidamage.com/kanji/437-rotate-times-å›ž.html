回 rotate / times

口 (mouth/small box radical) + (big box)
★★★★☆
6 strokes
JERK COUNTER
Literally, it means 'to rotate.' However, mostly you'll see it used to mean 'xxx amount of times.' I guess you could think of clock hands rotating around the clock, but it's still a JERK.

Onyomi

KAI

Mnemonic

Imagine a small box rotating inside a big box. like, I dunno, gears or something.
Then, use the rotating gears to reel in the string for that KITE, so you don't have to do it by hand.
