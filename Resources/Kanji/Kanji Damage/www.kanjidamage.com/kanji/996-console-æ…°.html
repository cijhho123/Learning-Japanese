慰 console

(corpse) + 示 (show/altar radical) + 寸 (glueglue) + (bottomheart)
★☆☆☆☆
15 strokes

Onyomi

I

Mnemonic

To console the grieving relatives at church, I glue the heart of the corpse to the altar (FYI it didn't work the way I planned)

慰安婦(いあんふ)

"comfort women" ☆☆☆☆☆ ABU
慰 (console) + 安 (cheap / safe) + 婦 (housewife / lady) = 慰安婦 ("comfort women")

the Korean 'comfort women' enslaved during the war, which Japan still won't make amends to.

compensation money, alimony. ☆☆☆☆☆
