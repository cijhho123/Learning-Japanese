駅 train station

馬 (horse) + 尺 (R for ROCK)
★★★★★
14 strokes

Onyomi

EKI

Mnemonic

Ride your horse to the train station to hear some rock music played by buskers outside.

★★★☆☆

Jukugo

駅員(えきいん)

train station staff. ★★★☆☆
駅 (train station) + 員 (clerk) = 駅員 (train station staff. )

各駅停車(かくえきていしゃ)

local train ★★☆☆☆
各 (each) + 駅 (train station) + 停 (bring to a halt) + 車 (car) = 各駅停車 (local train)

sure this is a scary and long word, but if you live here, you'll use it everyday： It means "local train" (literally, "stopping at each station train")
