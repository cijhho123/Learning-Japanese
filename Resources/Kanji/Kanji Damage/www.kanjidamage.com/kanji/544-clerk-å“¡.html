員 clerk

口 (mouth/small box radical) + 貝 (shellfish/money)
★★★☆☆
10 strokes
BETASUF
員 is a SUFFIX - it goes after any word for shop, to mean "a person who works in such a shop." For example,

Onyomi

IN

Mnemonic

When you give your money to the clerk IN the store, she uses her mouth to say thanks .
