Jukugo

虚栄(きょえい)

vanity ★★☆☆☆
虚 (empty) + 栄 (glory) = 虚栄 (vanity)

栄養(えいよう)

nutrition ★★☆☆☆
栄 (glory) + 養 (cultivate, rear) = 栄養 (nutrition)

光栄(こうえい)

it's an honor! ★☆☆☆☆ F
光 (shining) + 栄 (glory) = 光栄 (it's an honor!)

honor : usually used by lower person to higher person. "It's an honor to meet you, sir!" "I'm honored to have a chance to work with you, ma'am."
