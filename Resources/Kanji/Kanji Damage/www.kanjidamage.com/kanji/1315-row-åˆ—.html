列 row

(shhh) + 刀 (sword)
★★★☆☆
6 strokes
SOLOSTRONG

Onyomi

RETSU

Mnemonic

The Shogun says to the troops: Shhh!! Ten-hut! LET'S quiet down and stand in a row with our swords! It's time for an inspection.

Kunyomi

れつ a line of people or things which have been lined up. その列は、何ですか? means, 'What is this line for?' (it's for tickets to see ＱUEEN；THE MUSICAL) LAZY
