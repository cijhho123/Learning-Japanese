Onyomi

Mnemonic

Most GUYS already understand the general concept of trees. Even though they might not be able to explain photosynthesys in detail.

Jukugo

一概(いちがい) に

generalize ★★☆☆☆
一 (one) + 概 (general concept) = 一概 (generalize)

to make a sweeping generalization. (usually used in the negative: 一概には言えない (I don't want to generalize, but. . . ) )
概念(がいねん)

concept ★☆☆☆☆
概 (general concept) + 念 (concern) = 概念 (concept)
