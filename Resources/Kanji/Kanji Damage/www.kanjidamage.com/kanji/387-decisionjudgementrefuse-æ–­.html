断 decision/judgement/refuse

L (loser) + 米 (rice / America) + 斤 (axe)
★★★★☆
11 strokes
DUPE JERK
This means decision, but also means to judge, refuse, or cut off. Kind of related, but not really!

Onyomi

DAN

Mnemonic

Glen DANzig decided to cut up the loser (who stole his rice) with his axe.

Kunyomi

ことわ*る the most common word for 'refuse'
★★★☆☆
た＊つ to sever a thing, or to divide something in two.　Can also be used figuratively, as in 'Cut off all contact with so-and-so.' or 'Quit smoking altogether forever.'
★★☆☆☆

Jukugo

判断(はんだん) する

judge ★★★☆☆
判 (judgement) + 断 (decision/judgement/refuse) = 判断 (judge)

to judge, to deliver a verdict - but also judge like 'I wouldn't wear that skirt if I was her.'
診断(しんだん) する

diagnosis ★★☆☆☆
診 (diagnose) + 断 (decision/judgement/refuse) = 診断 (diagnosis)

diagnosis (診断する＝to diagnose)
油断(ゆだん) する

let down your guard. ★★☆☆☆
油 (oil) + 断 (decision/judgement/refuse) = 油断 (let down your guard.)
