Kunyomi

( を ) かえ*す I return something
★★★★★
( が ) かえ＊る something is returned or restored to its former state
★★★☆☆

Jukugo

お 返事(へんじ) する

reply ★★★★☆
返 (return or respond) + 事 (action/ incident) = 返事 (reply)

reply. In Japan, even if you respond to your friend's email 5 minutes after you got it, you have to start all mails with "Sorry my reply is so slow." (お返事が遅くてゴメン！）　（おへんじ　が　おそくて　ゴメン）
繰り返し(くりかえし)

to repeat over and over. ★☆☆☆☆
繰 (spin) + 返 (return or respond) = 繰り返し (to repeat over and over.)
