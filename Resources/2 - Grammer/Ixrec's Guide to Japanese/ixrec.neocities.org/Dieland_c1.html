(B D T)　すっすいやせん、おやっさん！
Breakdown:

Definitions:　 すいやせん = すみません = "sorry". The み becoming い is a slur common to all dialects, but using やす instead of ます is Kansai-ben.

(B D T)　いや、お客さんが大穴当てちまいやしてねぇ!?
Breakdown:

　　ちまう
　　　　いやす
　　　　　　して		 ねぇ !?
Definitions:　 いや = as a transition or lead-in, "no"/"well"/"actually"
お客さん = "guest"/"customer"/"client"
が marks a subject
大穴 = "dark horse". From Wikipedia: A dark horse is a race horse that is not known to gamblers and thus is difficult to place betting odds on.

Adapted Translation:　 It's just, a client won a bet on a dark horse, so I...
