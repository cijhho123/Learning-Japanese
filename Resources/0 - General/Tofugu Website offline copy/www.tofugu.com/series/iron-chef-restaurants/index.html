    Iron Chef Restaurant Review Battle Royale Pitting all the restaurants against each other in some kind of Iron Chef sequel

    5 articles in this series

    As huge fans of the TV series Iron Chef (and haters of the American version), we hold the skills of culinary giants like Hiroyuki Sakai and Chen Kenichi in high regard.

    And after years of seeing the food they prepared on TV, we set out to taste it for ourselves. During a trip to Japan, we visited a series of restaurants founded by former Iron Chefs… and judged the hell out of them.

    This is the Iron Chef sequel you didn't know existed. Whose cuisine reigned supreme on our taste buds? Read the reviews to find out, and then start planning which one you'll visit on your next trip to Japan.
