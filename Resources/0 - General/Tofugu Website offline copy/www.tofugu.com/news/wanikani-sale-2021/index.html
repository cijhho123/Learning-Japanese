    This Year's WaniKani Sale

    December 20, 2021 • words written

    It's that time of year again.

    You know what I'm talking about, right?

    When you stand in line to pick up a traditional fried chicken bucket from KFC.

    When you eat your Christmas cake.

    …Those are both good, but there's one other thing, too.

    It's also the season when WaniKani Lifetime accounts are 33% off.

    If you don't know what I'm talking about, this sale probably isn't for you. But let me fill you in anyways: WaniKani is Tofugu's system for learning kanji and Japanese vocabulary.

    You use mnemonics, SRS, and some very clever ordering to learn over 2,000 kanji and 6,000 vocabulary words. Some people do all this in about a year. We're always very impressed when people tell us that.

    If you're just learning about WaniKani, don't get this sale. Try it first. WaniKani isn't for everybody. It is, however, amazing for the right type of person. Maybe that's you, maybe it's not. You'll know when you get through the first three (free) levels of WaniKani. That's enough time to see if the spacing between reviews, and the mnemonics, are working for you.

    But if you are a current WaniKani resident, this sale is for you: $100 off a Lifetime membership! Use all that extra cash to feed you and your family KFC and Christmas cake for days.

    WaniKani Lifetime membership will be on sale from (approximately) December 20th, 2021 – January 5th, 2022. Hope to see you there.
