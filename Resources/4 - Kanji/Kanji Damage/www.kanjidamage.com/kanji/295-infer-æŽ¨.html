推 infer

(handleft) + (turkey)
★★☆☆☆
11 strokes
BETA

Onyomi

SUI

Judging from how much sugar the recipe calls for, I can infer that it will be SWEET.

Mnemonic

Even blindfolded, I can use my hands to infer that you are a turkey -- feeling your wattle especially gives me a weird thrill.

Jukugo

推測(すいそく) する

speculate. ★☆☆☆☆ NP
推 (infer) + 測 (scientific measurement) = 推測 (speculate.)

speculate. Kind of formal: "It's too early to speculate about the economy." "Scientists speculate that..."
推定(すいてい)

estimate ★☆☆☆☆ NP
推 (infer) + 定 (plan) = 推定 (estimate)

a guess, an estimate (i.e. estimate the price of a repair)
推理(すいり)

deductive reasoning, inference. ☆☆☆☆☆
推 (infer) + 理 (reason) = 推理 (deductive reasoning, inference.)
