異 to differ

田 (rice field) + 共 (with)
★★★☆☆
11 strokes

Onyomi

I

Ｉ　differ from P.Diddy

Mnemonic

A rice field has a difference with a plum field or a mango field.

Kunyomi

( ｘｘｘに ) こと*なる to differ according to xxx (similar to the ol' xxxによって違います）
★★★★☆
( xxxと ) こと＊なる use と to emphasize that, one thing is very specifically NOT like xxx.
★☆☆☆☆

Jukugo

異常(いじょう) な

abnormal ★★★☆☆
異 (to differ) + 常 (usual) = 異常 (abnormal)

abnormal (as in 'abnormal psychology')
同音異義語(どうおんいぎご)

homonym ★☆☆☆☆
同 (same) + 音 (sound) + 異 (to differ) + 義 (righteousness) + 語 (language) = 同音異義語 (homonym)

異性(いせい)

the opposite sex ☆☆☆☆☆
異 (to differ) + 性 (sex / essential nature) = 異性 (the opposite sex)
