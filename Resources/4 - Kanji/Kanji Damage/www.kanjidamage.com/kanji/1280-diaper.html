diaper

(big and right radical)
PK
Diaper is a strange radical because sometimes it is a "big" radical, with other radicals inside it, and sometimes it turns into a "right-side" radical. Be warned!!!! Diapers come in all sizes! Diaper () is different from 'Moustache'() because both of diaper's sides bend outward at the bottom. If you still have trouble telling them apart, think "FULL DIAPERS BULGE OUTWARDS".
