悔 regret

(leftheart) + 毎 (every)
★★★★☆
9 strokes
This isn't a moral regret- (that's more like 省みる (kaerimiru) or 後悔（koukai)). 悔しい is more like, you just regret that you didn't succeed in your evil schemes.

Onyomi

KAI

Charlie Brown regrets the time his KITE got eaten!

Mnemonic

I regret that I broke every heart I encountered

Kunyomi

くや＊しい this means, "Fuck! I almost had it and then it all went to hell GOD DAMN IT I WAS SO CLOSE!" 1/2 KANA
