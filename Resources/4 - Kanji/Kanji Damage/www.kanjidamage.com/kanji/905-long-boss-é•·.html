boss ★★★★★ SUF

SUFFIX meaning "Boss of XXX."
社長(しゃちょう)

company boss. ★★★★☆
社 (company) + 長 (long / boss) = 社長 (company boss.)

長所(ちょうしょ)

good point ★★☆☆☆
長 (long / boss) + 所 (area / attribute) = 長所 (good point)

strong point, as opposed to weak point. Can be used about people (his 長所 is his courage, her 長所 is her brain) or machines ('The strong point of this PC is its long battery-life')
身長(しんちょう)

height ★★☆☆☆
身 ((one's own) flesh) + 長 (long / boss) = 身長 (height)

height (BOOBOO; only about human bodies- not about buildings or, well, anything else)
延長(えんちょう) する

prolonging ★★☆☆☆
延 (prolong) + 長 (long / boss) = 延長 (prolonging)

extension or prolonging, in time. Also means,to extend a linear thing by building it longer. A bit more official than the related term 伸ばす　(のばす).
