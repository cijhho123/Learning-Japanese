small talk

大 (big) + (horny) + 日 (sun, day) + 小 (small size)
STRONGSYMBOLIC

SYMBOLIC : This is usually used in kanji about jobs or structured social get-togethers: That's why I called it small talk.

So you could say . . .

At our office we make small talk about who is big, who is horny, it's a sunny day ,and what office supplies to REORDER.
