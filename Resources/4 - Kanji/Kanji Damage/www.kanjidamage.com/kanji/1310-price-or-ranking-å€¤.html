merit ★★★☆☆
価 (value) + 値 (price or ranking) = 価値 (merit)

merit or worth - not used about money (it's worth it for the view! The good part of Dancehall is the bass!)

moral values ★★☆☆☆
価値 (merit) + 観 (point of view) = 価値観 (moral values)

One's moral values - used with specific things, like drinking, smoking, gambling.
