核 nucleus

木 (tree) + (pine cone)
★★☆☆☆
10 strokes

Onyomi

KAKU

Mnemonic

The pine cone is the nucleus of the tree, from which new trees start. Similarly, the COCK is the nucleus of the human male. (cf. every female stand-up comic)

clitoris ☆☆☆☆☆ SARC

clitoris - not a word you'd use in most conversations, but I'm including it anyway because the literal translation is so irresistable: hidden nucleus! How boss is that?
