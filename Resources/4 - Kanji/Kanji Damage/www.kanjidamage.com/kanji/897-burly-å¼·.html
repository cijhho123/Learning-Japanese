強 burly

弓 (bow) + ム (moocow) + 虫 (insizzect)
★★★★★
11 strokes
PN

Onyomi

KYOU

I learned a burly Kanji today (=今日, pronounced KYOU)

Mnemonic

The burly moocow used the bow to shoot the bug.

Kunyomi

し＊いる compel, or force someone to do something
★☆☆☆☆
( xxxが ) つよ＊い xxx is strong!
★★★★★

Jukugo

勉強(べんきょう) する

To study ★★★★★
勉 (try hard) + 強 (burly) = 勉強 (To study)

To study. Used about learning a whole subject, such as Irish History or Thermodynamics. (BOOBOO: 勉強 takes months or years, as opposed to 学ぶ　（まなぶ）　which means to learn one thing, and can take a second.)
強調(きょうちょう) する

emphasize ★★★☆☆
強 (burly) + 調 (check out) = 強調 (emphasize)

強制(きょうせい)

compulsory ★★☆☆☆ ABU
強 (burly) + 制 (system) = 強制 (compulsory)

compulsory, forced. "Do it, slave!"
強面(こわもて) の

rugged, thuggy ★☆☆☆☆ KUNKUN
強 (burly) + 面 (front surface / face) = 強面 (rugged, thuggy)

：someone who is rugged, thuggy, street-looking.
