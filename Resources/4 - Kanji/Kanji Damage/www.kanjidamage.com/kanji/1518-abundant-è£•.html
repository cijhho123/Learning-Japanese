裕 abundant

(ducky) + 谷 (valley/Swamp Thing radical)
★★★☆☆
12 strokes
BETAPN
Often dictionaries say that both 豊か　and 裕 mean 'ゆたか'. But that's silly. No one uses 裕, so don't worry about it.

Onyomi

YUU

Mnemonic

YOU can see duckies because they are abundant in this valley.

Jukugo

余裕(よゆう)

time/room to spare ★★★★☆
余 (excess) + 裕 (abundant) = 余裕 (time/room to spare)

room/time/money to spare. Like, don't sweat it.
