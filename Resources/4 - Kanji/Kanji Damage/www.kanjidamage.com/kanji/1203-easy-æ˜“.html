易 easy

日 (sun, day) + (thing)
★★★☆☆
8 strokes
DUPE

Onyomi

EKI, I

I went to the EKI (駅) easily because I had a map.

Mnemonic

The sun is an easy thing to see.

Kunyomi

やさ*しい easy,simple to do. (BOOBOO: don't mix it up with the other 'yasashii' (優し), which means kindhearted) 1/2 KANA
★★★☆☆

Jukugo

容易(ようい) に

easily ★★★☆☆

貿易(ぼうえき)

international trade ★★☆☆☆
貿 (international trading) + 易 (easy) = 貿易 (international trade)
