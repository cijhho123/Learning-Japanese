Jukugo

生殖(せいしょく) する

reproduction ★☆☆☆☆ NP
生 (life / birth) + 殖 (agricultural breeding, reproduction) = 生殖 (reproduction)

reproduction, as in 'reproductive organs,' or 'the reproductive process'
繁殖(はんしょく)

breeding ☆☆☆☆☆
繁 (multiple, many) + 殖 (agricultural breeding, reproduction) = 繁殖 (breeding)

breeding. Unlike the related term 生殖 （せいしょく）、繁殖 refers to the entire process of finding a mate, not just the physical act of insemination: Breeding grounds. Mating season. Farmers breeding livestock.
