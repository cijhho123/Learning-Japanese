得 bargain　/ obtain

(goleft) + 旦 (danna (husband)) + 寸 (glueglue)
★★★★☆
11 strokes
DUPE

Onyomi

TOKU

Mnemonic

Please go to obtain some TOKEs for my stoner husband, so he doesn't resort to sniffing glue.

Kunyomi

え*る to obtain (usually used with non-physical things: to obtain permission, or the right to do something, sentitive information, a patron, a prize or promotion )
★★★☆☆
( お ) とく (noun) a bargain, a good deal. Also means "benefit, advantage, profit." (not just money - like 'It pays to be on time' or 'It pays to be beautiful in this society') LAZY
★★★☆☆

Jukugo

説得(せっとく) する

persuade ★★★★☆
説 (explain) + 得 (bargain　/ obtain) = 説得 (persuade)

得意(とくい)

my forte! ★★☆☆☆
得 (bargain　/ obtain) + 意 (meaning) = 得意 (my forte!)

1) one's strong suit - the thing that someone does best (ｘｘｘ　が得意である！）

2) something one is proud of.（得意になった。)
