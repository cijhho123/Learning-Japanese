選 choose

(motion) + 己 (myself) + 共 (with)
★★★★☆
15 strokes

Onyomi

SEN

SEND for the chosen ones!! Bring them to me!

Mnemonic

Texas Governor Bush asked Dick Cheney to help him choose his running mate for the 2000 campaign. Dick cheney said, "OK, I make a motion to choose myself, with myself, to run with you."

Jukugo

選手(せんしゅ)

pro athlete ★★☆☆☆
選 (choose) + 手 (hand) = 選手 (pro athlete)

選択(せんたく) する

to choose ★★☆☆☆ F
選 (choose) + 択 (select) = 選択 (to choose)

choice- somewhat more formal than 選ぶ - you'd 選ぶ a bagel but 選択 a President.
選挙(せんきょ) する

an election ★★☆☆☆
選 (choose) + 挙 (raise / cite/ mention) = 選挙 (an election)
