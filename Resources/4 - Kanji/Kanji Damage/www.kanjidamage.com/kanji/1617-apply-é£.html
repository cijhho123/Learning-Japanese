遣 apply

虫 (insizzect) + 追 (follow)
★★☆☆☆
13 strokes
DUPE
Dictionaries say that both this　つかう and 　the OTHER つかう（使う) mean "to use". No. No!!! Not the case. 使う is the REAL "to use."

Mnemonic

つか*う to be careful or give consideration to. Usually only used with the phrase 気を遣う、 but even then it's usually hiragana so who cares. KANA
