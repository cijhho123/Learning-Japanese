実 truth

(crown) + (three Stooges)
★★★★★
8 strokes
PN

Onyomi

JITSU

Mnemonic

The truth is, homey's ju JITSU is so burly, he can take down 3 stooges. That's why he has the crown.

Kunyomi

じつ usually used like this：'実は xxx,' which is like saying 'The truth is...', as in, 'The truth is ,I used to be a dude named Carlos.' LAZY
★★★★★

Jukugo

実際(じっさい) に

in fact ★★★★☆
実 (truth) + 際 (edge) = 実際 (in fact)

in fact. (as in, 'You think you're Samoan, but in fact, you're adopted.')

Also has the nuance of 'in practice', as opposed to 'in theory.'
事実(じじつ)

fact ★★★★☆
事 (action/ incident) + 実 (truth) = 事実 (fact)

真実(しんじつ)

the truth ★★★★☆
真 (really) + 実 (truth) = 真実 (the truth)

現実(げんじつ)

reality ★★★☆☆
現 (reality) + 実 (truth) = 現実 (reality)
