麻 mah jongg / ganja

(cave) + 木 (tree)
★★☆☆☆
11 strokes
JERKBETASTRONG
(literally this means "hemp" but 90% of the time, you'll see it on the store-fronts of ma jongg parlors!)

The former Japanese Prime Minister's name (Aso) is 麻生, which literally means, 'hemp life.'

Onyomi

MA

Mnemonic

Two trees stand in a cave playing MAH jongg.
