縛 tie up

糸 (string) + 専 (specialty)
★★☆☆☆
16 strokes
DUPE

Onyomi

BAKU

Mnemonic

His specialty is tying up nuns with string.He ties their arms behind their BACK.

Kunyomi

しば*る To bind something - to tie a string AROUND something - to shackle a prisoner, to tie up a masochist, to put one's hair in a pony tail (BOOBOO: to tie a knot is 結ぶ　（むすぶ））. 1/2 KANA
★★☆☆☆

Jukugo

束縛(そくばく) する

restrain ★☆☆☆☆
束 (bundle of sticks) + 縛 (tie up) = 束縛 (restrain)

to restrain, to restrict, tie up in red tape.
