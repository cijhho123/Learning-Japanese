decapitated cow

ム (moocow) + 一 (one/line radical)
This is basically the lower half of moocow, plus a line on top, as if to say, "OMG, THAT COW GOT THE TOP CUT OFF!" After all, normally cows are quite round on top, or spikey if you include the horns.
