甲 turtle shell

申 (humbly say/god radical)
★☆☆☆☆
5 strokes
PK

Onyomi

KOU

as in, Shove a turtle shell up your COLON.

Mnemonic

It looks like the God radical(申）, but the vertical line only goes DOWN.

That's because the turtle's head(甲) goes DOWN into its shell when he's scared(but GOD　(申)is infinite so the line sticks OUT ON BOTH SIDEZZZ)
