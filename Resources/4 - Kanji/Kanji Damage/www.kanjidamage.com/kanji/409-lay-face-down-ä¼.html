伏 lay face down

(personleft) + 犬 (dog)
★☆☆☆☆
6 strokes

Onyomi

FUKU

He didn't want to lay face down because it would wrinkle his FUKU (服)

Mnemonic

The person orders the dog to lay face down.

Kunyomi

ふせ＊る to lay face down - also means, "to keep this a secret, to hide the truth from outsiders."
★☆☆☆☆

Jukugo

降伏(こうふく)

surrender ★☆☆☆☆
降 (descend /rainfall / get out of vehicle ) + 伏 (lay face down) = 降伏 (surrender)

surrender - unlike the more casual 降参　（こうさん）, 降伏 is used when you, like, lose a war.
