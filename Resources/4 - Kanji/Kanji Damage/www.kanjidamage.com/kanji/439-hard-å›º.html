固 hard

(big box) + 古 (old) Same-ON
★★★☆☆
8 strokes
DUPE

Onyomi

KO

That KODOMO (子) is hard to deal with.

Mnemonic

The old bread in the big box has gotten too hard to eat.

Kunyomi

かた＊い Hard like a stiff, formal, tight-ass person, stubborn.

Also means physically hard like firm: you tap it and it goes TON TON.
★★★★☆

( を ) かた＊める to make something congeal (clay, paint, pudding). OTHER MEANING: to harden or fortify one's defenses (or strengthen the unity of factions within the party).
★☆☆☆☆
( が ) かた＊まる something hardens, something takes shape
