縁 rim / omen

糸 (string) + (snout) + (pigbottom)
★★☆☆☆
15 strokes
JERKPN
JERK： it can mean rim or edge, but also a connection (think of ２ things joined at the edges).

Onyomi

EN

The rim has no END, you can keep going around it forever.

Mnemonic

Tie the string around the rim of the pig's snout, to keep it from biting.

Kunyomi

ふち rim or edge of table, cup, picture frame (emphasis is that it's not a point on the edge, it's the ENTIRE edge) KANA
★☆☆☆☆

Jukugo

縁起(えんぎ) のいい or の悪い

omen ★☆☆☆☆
縁 (rim / omen) + 起 (wake up / occur) = 縁起 (omen)

an omen (can be good or bad)
縁がある(えんがある)

to have a chance, to be linked by fate ★☆☆☆☆
