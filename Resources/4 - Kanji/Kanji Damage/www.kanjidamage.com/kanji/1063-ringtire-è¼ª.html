輪 ring/tire

車 (car) + (bookstore)
★★★☆☆
15 strokes

Onyomi

RIN

The wedding RING!

Mnemonic

My car needs tires to go to the bookstore.

Kunyomi

わ can mean ring, like rings of saturn, or loop, like cable which is stored in a loop, or circle, like folk-dances where everyone forms a circle. But 輪 is never an actual ring like you wear on your finger. NUBI
★☆☆☆☆

Jukugo

指輪(ゆびわ)

ring ★★★☆☆ KUNKUN
指 (finger / point at) + 輪 (ring/tire) = 指輪 (ring)

THIS is the ring you wear!
車輪(しゃりん)

tire ★★★☆☆
車 (car) + 輪 (ring/tire) = 車輪 (tire)

輪ゴム(わごむ)

rubber band ★★☆☆☆ KUNKUN
輪 (ring/tire) + ム () = 輪ゴム (rubber band)
