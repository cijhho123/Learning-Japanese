Jukugo

冷蔵庫(れいぞうこ)

the fuckin' fridge ★★★★☆
冷 (cold thing) + 蔵 (traditional storehouse) + 庫 (warehouse) = 冷蔵庫 (the fuckin' fridge )

冷静(れいせい) な

cool-headed ★★☆☆☆
冷 (cold thing) + 静 (quiet) = 冷静 (cool-headed)

cool-headed, calm person - someone who doesn't panic when the shit goes down.
