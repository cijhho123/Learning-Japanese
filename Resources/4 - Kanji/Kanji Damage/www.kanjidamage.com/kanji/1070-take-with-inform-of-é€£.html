Jukugo

連絡(れんらく) する

inform ★★★★★
連 (take with / inform of) + 絡 (get entangled) = 連絡 (inform)

to inform, to let someone know. Like, "Let me know if you're coming. Call me when you get there," kind of thing.
連続(れんぞく)

a series ★★★☆☆
連 (take with / inform of) + 続 (continue) = 連続 (a series)

に 関連している(かんれんしてる)

relevant ★☆☆☆☆
関 (connected to) + 連 (take with / inform of) = 関連している (relevant)

something that is relevant; something that seems related: two murders in the same week with both bodies' heads missing.
