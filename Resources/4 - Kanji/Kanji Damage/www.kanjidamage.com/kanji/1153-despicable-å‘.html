9 strokes

Onyomi

HI

Mnemonic

HE's such a despicable villain, he spit in 4 of my rice fields!!

Kunyomi

いや*しい foul, trifling,low, despicable - not used trivially. Used about child molesters and such.. Often used like this: 'XXXに いやしい',where XXX is the thing he's a dick about.
★★☆☆☆
いや＊しむべき contemptable, despicable　(literally, "ought to be despised.")
☆☆☆☆☆
いや＊しめる to despise someone. This is where it gets tricky. Only a bully or a dick いやしめるs someone.i.e. "Ha ha your parents got divorced!" or something.　I guess you could say, "Don't いやしめる or you will become いやしい."
★☆☆☆☆

Jukugo

卑怯(ひきょう) な

treacherous coward ★☆☆☆☆
卑 (despicable) + 怯 (get afeared) = 卑怯 (treacherous coward)

not just a coward ,but someone who does treacherous, trifling bullshit because they're a coward - like if they fight you they know they can't win fairly so they'll knee your groin, they're a 卑怯なmotherfucker.
卑下(ひげ) する

put yourself down ☆☆☆☆☆
卑 (despicable) + 下 (below) = 卑下 (put yourself down)

to put yourself down (BOOBOO: only used when talking to others - mumbling "I suck!" to yourself doesn't count for some reason)
