曽 gain

(right radical) (horny) + 田 (rice field) + 日 (sun, day)
STRONGOBSOLETE
strong RADICAL: ZOU.

I GAIN HORNYness watching a shirtless ZORRO sweat in the RICE PADDY under the SUN.

OBSOLETE KANJI: officially this means “former," but it is rarely used, so we're going to use it to mean “gain," for reasons that will become clear in one second...
