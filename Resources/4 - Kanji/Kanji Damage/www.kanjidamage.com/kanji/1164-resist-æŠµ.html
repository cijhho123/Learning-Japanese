抵抗(ていこう) する

resist ★★★☆☆
抵 (resist) + 抗 (oppose) = 抵抗 (resist)

to resist, used about one person taking on the whole fuckin' system, mannn!: draft resister, resisting arrest, La Resistance.

Also, to encounter resistance, resist temptation, and the electric concept of 'resistance' are also included in the meaning.
