食 eat

(peaked roof) + 良 (good)
★★★★★
9 strokes
SYMBOLIC
SYMBOLIC 2/3 of the time.

Onyomi

SHOKU

I was SHOCKED when the dude ate like Jello with eels in it.

Mnemonic

It's good to eat in a restaurant with a peaked roof. Shows class.

Kunyomi

たべ*る eat
★★★★★
く＊う to devour!!! it's kind of a more mean way of saying 'to eat.'- most of the negative, dark expressions involving eating use KUU rather than TABERU. Also - very seldom - it means to make a living, as in the English phrase, 'You gotta work to eat.'
★★☆☆☆

Jukugo

食べ物(たべもの)

food ★★★★★
食 (eat) + 物 (animal / thing) = 食べ物 (food)

food. Can be used of the ingredients OR the finished, cooked product.

western food ★★☆☆☆
