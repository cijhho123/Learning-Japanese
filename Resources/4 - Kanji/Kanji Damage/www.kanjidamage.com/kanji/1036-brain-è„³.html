脳 brain

月 (moon/organ) + 凶 (terrible) + (t.c.f.b.b.y.e.s.i.y.l.)
★★☆☆☆
11 strokes
BETA

Onyomi

NOU

Mnemonic

The 3 dots are usually Cutest Baby Birds, but in this case let's call them hair, covering your brain.
Your brain is NO good. It's just a terrible organ, covered in greasy hair.
