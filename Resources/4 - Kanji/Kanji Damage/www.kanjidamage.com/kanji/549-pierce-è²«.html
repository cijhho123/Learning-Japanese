貫 pierce

母 (your Moms) + 貝 (shellfish/money)
★☆☆☆☆
11 strokes

Onyomi

KAN

Mnemonic

His mother gave Genghis KHAN some money to get fashionable piercings.

Kunyomi

つらぬ＊く to penetrate all the way through: not just violently. (for example, 'The river runs through the town.') Also to see a project all the way through to the end KANA
★☆☆☆☆

Jukugo

一貫(いっかん) して

consistency ☆☆☆☆☆
一 (one) + 貫 (pierce) = 一貫 (consistency)

to do something consistently all the way till it is done. (一貫性　（いっかんせい）= consistency)
