Jukugo

傾向(けいこう)

tendency ★☆☆☆☆
傾 (lean) + 向 (turn to face) = 傾向 (tendency)

a general tendency or demographic trend (not one specific person and not with any intention) (i.e. people who watch 8 hours of tv a day tend to drool) (BOOBOO: not used about fashion trends, which are conscious and intentional, and called 流行 (りゅうこう))
