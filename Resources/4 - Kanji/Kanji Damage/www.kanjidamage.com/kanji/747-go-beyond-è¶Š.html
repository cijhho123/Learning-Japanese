越 go beyond

走 (run) + 成 (become)
★★★☆☆
12 strokes
DUPEILL PAIR
Unlike the OTHER こす　（超す） , which means go OVER (a number or limit), THIS 越す means go BEYOND - physically.

Onyomi

Mnemonic

The Nazis ran BEYOND the limits of good taste and became extinct.

Kunyomi

( を ) こ＊す I go beyond (a physical thing) - I pass or crosss something (a lake, a city, etc)
★★★☆☆
( が ) こ＊える something crosses something else
★★★☆☆

Jukugo

引っ越す(ひっこす)

move to a new house or apartment ★★★★☆
引 (pull) + 越 (go beyond) = 引っ越す (move to a new house or apartment)

追い越す(おいこす)

overtake ★★★☆☆
追 (follow) + 越 (go beyond) = 追い越す (overtake)
