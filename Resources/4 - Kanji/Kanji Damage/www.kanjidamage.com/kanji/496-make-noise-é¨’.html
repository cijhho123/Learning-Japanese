騒 make noise

馬 (horse) + 又 (again/crotch radical) + 虫 (insizzect)
★★★☆☆
18 strokes

Onyomi

SOU

Mnemonic

The horse made SO much noise when all its crotch-bugs bit it at once.

Kunyomi

( に ) さわ＊ぐ Make noise．　As in,　road construction noise, and also as in　high school students laughing at dumb shit at the next table．Most commonly used in the phrase: ギャーギャー騒ぐ (no particle needed)
★★★☆☆
