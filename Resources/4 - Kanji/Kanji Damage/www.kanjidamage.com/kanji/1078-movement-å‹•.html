動 movement

重 (heavy / overlap) + 力 (strong)
★★★★★
11 strokes

Onyomi

DOU

As Homer tries to move the Stonehenge, he grunts, "DOH!"

Mnemonic

Kunyomi

( を ) うご*く I move； he moves himself.

automatic ★★★★☆

動詞(どうし)

verb ★★★☆☆
動 (movement) + 詞 (part of speech) = 動詞 (verb)
