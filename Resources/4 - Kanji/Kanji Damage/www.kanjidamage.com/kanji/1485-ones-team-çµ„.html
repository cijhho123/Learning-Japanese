組 one's team

糸 (string) + (Abe Lincoln's Hat) Same-ON
★★★★☆
11 strokes
CCW
Cultural Code Word; According to Alex Kerr's seminal Japan-rant DOGS AND DEMONS, kids are assigned a kumi from the first day of school, and spend their whole lives with their friends basically chosen by institutions (school, then university clubs, then co-workers).

Of course this happens everywhere, but it takes Japan to on-purpose systematically do it, and even give it a name.

The good side is, you'll always have pals even if you have nothing in common besides the same boss. The bad side is, you'll never be able to make friends independently because you were never taught / given the opportunity to develop the social tools necessary to do so.

This whole phenomenon is related to another CCW, amae .

Onyomi

SO

I need Some One on my team - I can't do it alone.

Mnemonic

Our team is all wearing Abe hats which are tied together with string. (so we can't do anything on our own - this is Japan, after all)
