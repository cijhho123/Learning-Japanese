抱 hug

(handleft) + (wrap) Same-ON
★★★★☆
8 strokes
SOLOCOCK
You'd think that 'hug' would be the nicest kanji, but no, it's another COCK.

Both kunyomi have the same particle (を) AND the same okurigana (く), but are pronounced differently . . . so it's impossible to tell which one it is!

Luckily, one of the kunyomi is very very rarely used, so you can just assume it's the other one. Sigh.

Onyomi

HOU

He's a christian, so he'll just hug HOES instead of fornicating them.

Mnemonic

You hug by wrapping your hands around someone.

Kunyomi

( を　or が ) かか＊える to embrace, to grasp
