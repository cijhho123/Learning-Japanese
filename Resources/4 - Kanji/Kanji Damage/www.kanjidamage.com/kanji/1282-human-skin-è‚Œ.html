肌 human skin

月 (moon/organ) + (diaper)
★★☆☆☆
6 strokes

Onyomi

Mnemonic

Your skin is like a diaper to keep blood from leaking out of the body's organs.

Kunyomi

はだ refers to the skin of a person - not to leather or animal skin (BOOBOO)

texture ★☆☆☆☆

the texture of something (i.e. how it feels when rubbed on your skin)
