渇 thirsty

(waterleft) + 日 (sun, day) + 匂 (scent (good or bad))
★★☆☆☆
11 strokes
DUPESOLO

Onyomi

Mnemonic

Yo Mama so inept, when she gets thirsty in the sun, she starts sniffing around for the scent of water.

Kunyomi

( が ) かわ*く something which is/should be wet , becomes dry: My eyes are dry. My throat is dry. Usually only used when you are thirsty, as in: のどが渇いてる!のど が かわいてる! I'm thirsty!
