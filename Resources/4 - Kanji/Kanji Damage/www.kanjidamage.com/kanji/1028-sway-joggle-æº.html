揺 sway, joggle

(handleft) + (nest) + 缶 (can, like 'canned beef')
★★☆☆☆
12 strokes

Onyomi

YOU

She YODELS so loud it'll make a skyscraper sway.

Mnemonic

The '缶' radical is somewhat simplified, but let's roll with it anyway, because we can say:

He holds out his hand, holding a tin can with a nest on top of it, and jiggles it so the nest sways.

Kunyomi

( を ) ゆ＊さぶる I shake or shock something : I make something sway
★★★☆☆
( が ) ゆ＊れる something gets a jolt, something sways
★★★☆☆
( を ) ゆ＊する to shock , jolt, to make something sway or shake
★☆☆☆☆

Jukugo

動揺(どうよう) する

jolt ☆☆☆☆☆
動 (movement) + 揺 (sway, joggle) = 動揺 (jolt)

to be agitated or unsettled, to be jolted
