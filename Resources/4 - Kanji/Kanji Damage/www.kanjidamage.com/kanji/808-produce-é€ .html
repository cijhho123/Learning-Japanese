造 produce

(motion) + 告 (inform)
★★★☆☆
10 strokes
DUPE

Onyomi

ZOU

An ELEPHANT (象= ZOU) produces other elephants.

Mnemonic

The motion of the factory machines informs you that they are in production.

Kunyomi

つく*る to mass-produce something in a factory. (although one-of-a-kind things like a giant rocket ship are also 造る)
★★★☆☆

Jukugo

製造(せいぞう)

manufacturing ★★☆☆☆ NP
製 (mass production) + 造 (produce) = 製造 (manufacturing)

manufacturing, production
改造(かいぞう)

remodeling, renewal ★★☆☆☆
改 (renew / improve) + 造 (produce) = 改造 (remodeling, renewal)

remodeling, renewal, or reorganization　of a physical thing ; re-modeling a house, tricking out a car, re-organizing the leadership of an organization.
創造的(そうぞうてき) な

creative ★★☆☆☆
創 (originate / creative) + 造 (produce) + 的 (motivation / '-al') = 創造的 (creative)

creative (BOOBOO: don't confuse it with the OTHER そうぞう：想像, which means 'imagine.') (I know, it's pernicious!)
