採 gather

(handleft) + (nest) + 木 (tree)
★★☆☆☆
11 strokes
DUPE

Onyomi

SAI

Mnemonic

I SIGH as I gather with my hand the nest from the tree (the eggs of the last dodo are inside - all eaten from within by hideous gibbering parasites).

Kunyomi

と*る gather (mushrooms, cherries, honey (if you are a bee)). Also, sometimes, it means to adopt a certain policy or take a certain measure.
★★☆☆☆

Jukugo

採用(さいよう) する

adopt ★☆☆☆☆
採 (gather) + 用 (utilize) = 採用 (adopt)

adopt a policy or resolution
伐採(ばっさい)

logging ☆☆☆☆☆

cutting down a tree. Logging.
