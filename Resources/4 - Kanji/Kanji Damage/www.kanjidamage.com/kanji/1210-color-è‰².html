色 color

(bait) + 一 (one/line radical)
★★★★★
6 strokes
COCK
This guy sucks. In jukugo, it uses the kunyomi half the time, and even when it DOES use the onyomi like it's supposed to, it has TWO onyomi which are used half the time randomly. Jesus.

The only hint I can give you is this: in the NAMES of colors, 色 tends to use the KUN, while in other jukugo it uses the traditional ON. Usually. If it wants to.

Onyomi

SHIKI, SHOKU

SHE KEEPS wearing the most SHOCking colors.

Mnemonic

bait worm PLUS a curved line. . . Imagine that that line is the smile of a fish, ensnared by the bright colors of the bait worm.
