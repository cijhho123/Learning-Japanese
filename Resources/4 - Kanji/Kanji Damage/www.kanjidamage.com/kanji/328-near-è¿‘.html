に 近寄る(ちかよる)

get close to ★★☆☆☆
近 (near) + 寄 (get close) = 近寄る (get close to)

to get close to, to approach.(usually used in the negative, on warning signs: danger! keep out! danger! high voltage! 近寄るな!)
近所(きんじょ)

around here ★★☆☆☆
近 (near) + 所 (area / attribute) = 近所 (around here)

in this neighborhood, around here- (BOOBOO: 近所 is strictly relative to where you are standing at the time. You can't say 'Shibuya is a neighborhood in tokyo,' - in that case, you have to use MACHI(町)。)
に or が 近付く(ちかづく)

get close to destination ★★☆☆☆ 1/2 KANA
近 (near) + 付 (stick to) = 近付く (get close to destination)

to draw close to a physical destination or a goal.
