超 exceed/ go over

召 (summon) + 走 (run)
★★★★☆
12 strokes
DUPEILL PAIR
This means 'exceed a numerical value'. Not like exceed one's patience or my band exceeds your band.

Onyomi

CHOU

Margaret CHOU goes over a tall wall in search of a Snickers.

Mnemonic

Summon the courage to run farther than you have ever run before, go over the hills and mountains!

Kunyomi

( を ) こ＊える to exceed or go over a certain limit (the cook heated the oven to over 100 degrees, the dude passed 100 years old, the man bet over 40,000 dollars, etc.)
★★★★☆
( also を ) こ＊す exceed, to go past　（ａ　ｃｉｔｙ，　ａ　ｒｉｖｅｒ　，　ｅｔｃ） also go past in time (survive the winter) or surpass something (wins surpass losses, profits surpass debts).
★★★★☆
