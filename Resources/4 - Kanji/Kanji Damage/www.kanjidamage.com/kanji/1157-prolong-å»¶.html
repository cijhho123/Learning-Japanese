延 prolong

正 (correct) + (buildbottom)
★★★☆☆
8 strokes
DUPE

Onyomi

EN

If a guy's schlong is getting prolonged, you can tell that he's ENjoying something.

Mnemonic

If you want me to build this building correctly, you'll have to prolong the deadline.

Kunyomi

の＊ばす to lengthen or extend (the life of a sick person)(the deadline) (your stay in a hotel). Also, to procrastinate. More used with time than physical length.
★★★☆☆

Jukugo

延長(えんちょう) する

prolonging ★★☆☆☆
延 (prolong) + 長 (long / boss) = 延長 (prolonging)

extension or prolonging, in time. Also means,to extend a linear thing by building it longer. A bit more official than the related term 伸ばす　(のばす).
延期(えんき) する

postpone ★★☆☆☆
延 (prolong) + 期 (period of time) = 延期 (postpone)

生き延びる(いきのびる)

outlive ★☆☆☆☆
生 (life / birth) + 延 (prolong) = 生き延びる (outlive)

to outlive others, to survive a fucked event
