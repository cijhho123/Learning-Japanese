家 home

(crown) + (pigbottom)
★★★★★
10 strokes
STRONGSUF
Often used as a sufix for jobs, usually jobs that can be done from home, like comic book artist (漫画家（まんがか））

Onyomi

KA

Drive home in your CAR, ok?

Mnemonic

Why is that pig wearing a crown in our home? That's no pig, that's my father!

家族(かぞく)

family ★★★★★
家 (home) + 族 (family) = 家族 (family)

your immediate family - parents, grandparents, siblings.
政治家(せいじか)

politician ★★★☆☆
政治 (politics) + 家 (home) = 政治家 (politician)

作家：(さっか)

author ★★★☆☆
作 (make) + 家 (home) = 作家： (author)

家庭(かてい)

the family you are living with. ★★☆☆☆
家 (home) + 庭 (garden) = 家庭 (the family you are living with.)

専門家(せんもんか)

specialist ★☆☆☆☆ SARCSUF
専門 (a specialty) + 家 (home) = 専門家 (specialist)

a specialist (often shortened to just ｘｘｘ専)

（this is often used sarcastically - for instance a デブ専 is a "fat person specialist", meaning a "chubby chaser.")
