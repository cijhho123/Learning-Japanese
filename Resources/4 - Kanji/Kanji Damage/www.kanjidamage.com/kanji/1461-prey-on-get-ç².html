獲 prey on　/ get

(catleft) + (flowertop) + (turkey) + 又 (again/crotch radical)
★☆☆☆☆
16 strokes
DUPE

Onyomi

KAKU

Mnemonic

The cat preys on a turkey whose crotch has a COCK attached

Kunyomi

え＊る get or obtain :Unlike the OTHER える(得る ), 獲る is used for　 hunting - the predator obtains prey. Also sometimes used about sports victories.
★☆☆☆☆

Jukugo

獲得(かくとく) する

acquire ★★☆☆☆
獲 (prey on　/ get) + 得 (bargain　/ obtain) = 獲得 (acquire)

to obtain. Very similar to 獲る　（える） but 獲得 has the connotation of "obtain with great effort", or "FINALLY obtain." 獲る is more like, obtain something easy or small.
獲物(えもの)

prey ★☆☆☆☆ KUNKUN
獲 (prey on　/ get) + 物 (animal / thing) = 獲物 (prey)
