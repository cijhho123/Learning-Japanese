権 rights

木 (tree) + (swan)
★★★★☆
15 strokes
BETA

Onyomi

KEN

KENNY has the right to come back from the dead.

Mnemonic

The swan has a right to peck the tree.

Jukugo

人権(じんけん)

human rights ★★★★☆
人 (person) + 権 (rights) = 人権 (human rights )

権利(けんり)

the right to do ★★★☆☆
権 (rights) + 利 (handy) = 権利 (the right to do)

the authority or right to do something
権威(けんい)

authority ★★☆☆☆
権 (rights) + 威 (terrible majesty) = 権威 (authority)

an authority one has based on power alone (parents, cops, etc.) rather than on rights (authority based on rights is　権利（けんり））
権力(けんりょく)

power ★☆☆☆☆
権 (rights) + 力 (strong) = 権力 (power)

power (over other people)(as in boss, parent, governor, pope, etc.)
