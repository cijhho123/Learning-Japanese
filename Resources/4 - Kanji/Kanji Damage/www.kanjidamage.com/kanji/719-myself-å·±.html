己 myself

★★☆☆☆
3 strokes
PKSTRONG
Although the onyomi for this is KO, it is STRONG FOR 'KI', - which is to say, most of the kanji using this as a radical have the onyomi of KI for some reason!

Onyomi

KO

Mnemonic

The sound of the word KO makes you think of kodomo(子), and 己 looks like a snake, so you could say:

I myself, am a baby snake.

Kunyomi

おのれ myself- a very LITERARY way of saying 私 F

introduce yourself to a group. ★★☆☆☆
