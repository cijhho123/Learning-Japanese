満 full

(waterleft) + (flowertop) + 両 (both)
★★★★★
12 strokes

Onyomi

MAN

The MAN was full of pie.

Mnemonic

Both flowers were full of rain- water.

Kunyomi

( を ) み＊たす to fill up, either literally or metaphorically (e.g. to satisfy or fulfill)
★☆☆☆☆

Jukugo

満足(まんぞく) する

to be totally satisfied. ★★★★☆
満 (full) + 足 (foot/ be enough) = 満足 (to be totally satisfied.)

満員(まんいん)

all full up! ★★☆☆☆
満 (full) + 員 (clerk) = 満員 (all full up!)

the event is full, there's no seats left
満月(まんげつ)

full moon ★★☆☆☆ SARCNEOABU
満 (full) + 月 (moon) = 満月 (full moon)

full moon. Also, when you moon someone with your ass, that is also a mangetsu.
肥満(ひまん)

obesity ★☆☆☆☆
肥 (obese / manure) + 満 (full) = 肥満 (obesity)

豊満(ほうまん) な

voluptuous ★☆☆☆☆ ABU
豊 (plentiful) + 満 (full) = 豊満 (voluptuous)

not what it sounds like! Houman means zatfig, voluptuous. This has two meanings： a) a borderline sexual-harassment word used generally about the big titty women. b) a a relatively polite way to describe a chubby woman. Anyway, ABU!
