SIDEKICK

KYOU

Mnemonic

KYOU (今日), my older brother's condition is he is wet . He just got out of the shower.

Jukugo

状況(じょうきょう)

context, condition ★★★★★
状 (circumstance) + 況 (condition) = 状況 (context, condition)

the state of affairs, the condition, the context: どういう状況でその言葉を使う？ (どういう　じょうきょう　で　その　ことぼあ　を　つかう？ = In what situation do you use that word?)

recession ★☆☆☆☆

economic slump or recession; Republican administration
