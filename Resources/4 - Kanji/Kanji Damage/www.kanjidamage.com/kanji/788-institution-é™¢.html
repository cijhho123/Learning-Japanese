院 institution

(town) + 完 (complete)
★★☆☆☆
10 strokes
BETA

Onyomi

IN

for once the onyomi actually SOUNDS like the English keyword! INnnnnnnnnstitution!

Mnemonic

IN our town, we have completed our IN-stitutions = we have a library, a town hall and a university.

Jukugo

病院(びょういん)

hospital ★★★★☆
病 (sick kanji) + 院 (institution) = 病院 (hospital)

美容院(びよういん)

beauty salon. ★★☆☆☆
美 (beauty) + 容 (appearance) + 院 (institution) = 美容院 (beauty salon.)

beauty salon. Yes, hospital （びょういん） and beauty salon (びよういん） DO sound totally alike.This is one of the basic mindfucks that teachers like to spring on newbies, so act like you know.
