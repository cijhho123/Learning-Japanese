学 knowledge

(mohawk) + 子 (child)
★★★★★
8 strokes

Onyomi

GAKU

Mnemonic

The mohican child had a major knowledge of punk, and that was how he stopped being a square and GOT COOL.

Kunyomi

まな*ぶ 2 meanings: to learn something from a teacher. Also, to learn one random fact (not a whole subject). Unlike 勉強、 you can't 学ぶ by yourself.
★★★★★
