掛 hang / halfway done

(handleft) + (termite mound) Same-ON + (katakana toe)
★★★☆☆
11 strokes
DUPE

Onyomi

GAI

Mnemonic

Use your hand to hang the GUY's toe from the termite mound.

The termites will feast tonight! (the hand was made out of wood, and the guy was a pirate - it's a long story).

Kunyomi

( を ) か＊ける I hang something using a hook or pin. Also, to sprinkle something on something else (pepper, salt, etc.) Also means to drape over : drape a blanket over a sleeping child or put a tablecloth on the um uh table. Also means to turn on something electric, although how this is different from 使う is unclear!
