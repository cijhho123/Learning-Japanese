玉 ball

王 (king) + 丶 (dot)
★★★★☆
5 strokes
DUPESOLO

Onyomi

Mnemonic

The middle line of "king 王" is his arm, and the little dot is his ballsac.

The king likes playing with his testicles.

Kunyomi

たま (broad meaning) any ball - a fire-ball, ball bearing, a mathematical sphere, a glass bead. Also it has the nuance of gem, or jewel.
★★★☆☆
