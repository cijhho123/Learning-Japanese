Mnemonic

A hellbunny was fighting a young lady, so JOE Stalin kicked him six times in the nuts. (woman is also a radical, but you don't need a mnemonic to make the connection!)

Kunyomi

( を ) じょう ( さん ) young lady - a bit formal. Has the nuance of an eligible single daughter of a rich family. A flattering word used when picking up girls. Usually used on women up to 20 years old, but if you're 70 you can use it to a 40 year old single woman. F
