(handleft) + 旦 (danna (husband))
★★★★☆
8 strokes

Mnemonic

Kunyomi

Jukugo

担当(たんとう) する

be responsible for ★★★☆☆
担 (carry on your back) + 当 (hit the target) = 担当 (be responsible for)

to be in charge of; to be responsible for
負担(ふたん)

financial responsibility ☆☆☆☆☆
負 (lose) + 担 (carry on your back) = 負担 (financial responsibility)

a responsibility, usually financial, which is declared by a system. "If a guest falls off the hotel roof, it's the hotel's responsibility." "The parent is responsible for paying for the child's lunch at boarding school."
