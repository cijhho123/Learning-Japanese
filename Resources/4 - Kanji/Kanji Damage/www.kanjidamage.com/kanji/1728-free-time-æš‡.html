暇 free time

日 (sun, day) + (FFFFFoolio) + 口 (mouth/small box radical) + 又 (again/crotch radical)
★★★☆☆
13 strokes
SOLO

Onyomi

KA

Mnemonic

On a sunny day, if I have the free time, me and Foolio go on a CAR ride. He's rapping with his mouth, and I'm grabbing my crotch.

Kunyomi

ひま free - not busy. People sometimes use this sarcastically, as if to say, "He has too much time on his hands." or "Get a life." 暇だね！ SARC1/2 KANA
