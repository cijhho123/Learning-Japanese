優 kindhearted/excel

(personleft) + 憂 (grieve) Same-ON
★★★★☆
17 strokes
JERKDUPEPN
JERK: by itself, it means nice, but in a JUKUGO, it means EXCEL, or, for some reason, ACTOR.

Also this kanji has the distinction of being a DOUBLE dupe: there's two やさしいs AND two すぐれるs!

Onyomi

YUU

Mnemonic

YOU are such a kindhearted person to grieve for the loss of my mom's cousin's great-aunt twice removed, whom you've never even met!

In other words, a kindhearted PERSON often excels at grief.

Kunyomi

( に or が ) ずぐ＊れる to outdo, to be superior.
★★☆☆☆
やさ＊しい warm-hearted. , emphasis on the personality, They make people feel comfortable and understand the feelings of others. Young people can use 優しい人 as kind of a put-down, like "He's just a 'nice guy.'"
★★★★★
