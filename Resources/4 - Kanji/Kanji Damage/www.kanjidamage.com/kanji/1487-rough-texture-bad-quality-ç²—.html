粗品(そしな)

a lame gift ★★★☆☆ FPF
粗 (rough texture / bad quality) + 品 (products) = 粗品 (a lame gift)

a lame gift - by law no matter what you give someone, you have to apologize to them for not giving a better gift. You say,粗品ですが、どうぞ。
