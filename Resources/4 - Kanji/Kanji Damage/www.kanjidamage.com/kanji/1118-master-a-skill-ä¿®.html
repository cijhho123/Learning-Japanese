修 master a skill

(personleft) + (director) + (hair) + 一 (one/line radical)
★★☆☆☆
10 strokes
SIDEKICK

Onyomi

SHUU

Gene Simmons is a master of walking in high-heel SHOES.

Mnemonic

next to 人 is a single vertical line, which let's think of as "a straight razor" the person is holding.

Thus:

That person is a master of shaving hair with a straight razor, so they made him the director of the barbershop!

Jukugo

修理(しゅうり) する

fix ★★★☆☆
修 (master a skill) + 理 (reason) = 修理 (fix)

to fix a thing- （BOOBOO: you don't 直す a car, or a computer, you 修理 it)
研修(けんしゅう)

study, training ☆☆☆☆☆
研 (polish, sharpen) + 修 (master a skill) = 研修 (study, training )
