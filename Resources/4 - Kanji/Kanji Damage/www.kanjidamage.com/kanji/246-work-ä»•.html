仕事(しごと)

one's job, one's work. ★★★★★ KUN ON
仕 (work) + 事 (action/ incident) = 仕事 (one's job, one's work. )

仕方(しかた)

way to do something ★★★★☆
仕 (work) + 方 (direction / method / person) = 仕方 (way to do something)

Most often used in the negative: 'Shikata ga nai.' as in, 'fuck it.'
