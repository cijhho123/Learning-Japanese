隔 segregate

(town) + 口 (mouth/small box radical) + ??? (???)
★★☆☆☆
13 strokes

Onyomi

KAKU

Mnemonic

That town is a bunch of segregationist COCKS.

Kunyomi

へだ＊てる to partition, to separate: separate the class into "gifted" and "not gifted" students. Partition the room using folding screens. Divide the property between people. 5mずつ隔てて： every five feet (there's a pillar or fencepost).
★★☆☆☆

Jukugo

隔離(かくり) する

quarantine / put in internment camps ★☆☆☆☆
隔 (segregate) + 離 (divorce / physical distance between things) = 隔離 (quarantine / put in internment camps)

to quarantine people - or 隔離背策：(かくりせいさく)　an official policy to keep people in concentration camps, like USA did to Japanese-Americans during the war, or like Israel is doing in Palestine.
隔週(かくしゅう) xxx

every other xxx ★☆☆☆☆ PRE
隔 (segregate) + 週 (week) = 隔週 (every other xxx)

PREFIX:every other (week, month, year, etc.)
間隔(かんかく)

small gap ☆☆☆☆☆
間 (a period of time) + 隔 (segregate) = 間隔 (small gap)

a small physical distance or gap - a literal space between two things: between two close-together buildings, say, or between the train and the platform.
