貝 shellfish/money

(bottom radical) 目 (eye) + (animal legs)
★★★★★
7 strokes
SOLOJERK RADICALSYMBOLIC
JERK KANJI ALERT - by itself it means shellfish, but when combined with another kanji to form a compound word, it means "money". They say that shellfish used to be kind of like "wampum" before gold was discovered.

Onyomi

Mnemonic

You look at money. You sell animal legs for money.

Kunyomi

かい shell
