産 childbirth, production of things

立 (stand up) + (cliff) + 生 (life / birth)
★★★★☆
11 strokes
DUPEFR
a bit of a FR (women are child factories? According to Japanese politicians, yes.)

Onyomi

SAN

Mnemonic

SANTA Claus stands on top of the cliff and produces toys, giving them life, and then they roll downhill into your chimney.

Kunyomi

う*む to give birth (human or animal) .This is more of a medical or agricultural word. Normally we use the OTHER うむ　（生む） for everyday childbirth. NP
