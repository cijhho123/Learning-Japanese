確認(かくにん) する

double-check ★★★★☆
確 (make certain) + 認 (admit) = 確認 (double-check)

to double-check something.
確率(かくりつ)

probability ★★☆☆☆
確 (make certain) + 率 (ratio) = 確率 (probability)

確実(かくじつ)

certainty ☆☆☆☆☆ NP
確 (make certain) + 実 (truth) = 確実 ( certainty)

certainty. This is never used as a verb. You'd never say 確実する。
