要点(ようてん)

the important points ★★☆☆☆
要 (important) + 点 (point) = 要点 (the important points)

the important points (unlike the related word 重点（じゅうてん）、　要点 is more than one point - more like a summary. Like instead of writing an essay, you'd just do a series of bullet points.
Example phrase:要点だけ話して！　("Get to the point!")

需要(じゅよう)

demand ★★☆☆☆ NP
需 (demand) + 要 (important) = 需要 (demand)

demand in the economic sense - supply and demand

This does NOT get to turn into a verb. You don't use する. You'd say 需要がある (there's a demand for xxx) or perhaps　　需要を　満たす　（じゅようをもたす） (there's enough of xxx to meet the demand).
