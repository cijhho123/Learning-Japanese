闘 struggle

★★☆☆☆

Mnemonic

Kunyomi

( と ) たたか*う fight - (only used figuratively) - struggle against temptation, war on poverty, the, uh, the uh, hold on, the effin' war on drugs, struggle against prejudice, etc.
