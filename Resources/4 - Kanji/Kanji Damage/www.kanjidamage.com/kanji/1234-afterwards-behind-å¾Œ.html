後 afterwards / behind

(goleft) Same-ON + (Eazy) + (eachbottom)
★★★★★
9 strokes
COCK
COCK: it has 3 totally different KUN-yomi - 2 of which have NO OKURIGANA to help you tell them apart!

In a pinch, you can guess the pronunciation by checking the particles:あと goes with で but のち goes with に。 Usually.

Onyomi

GO, KOU

Mnemonic

Each time you GO see Eazy-E rap, afterwards you buy some COCAINE from him.

Kunyomi

( xxxの ) あと ( で ) after. Usually used like this: XXXの＊あと, or simply あとで！
★★★★★
( YYYの ) のち ( に ) after YYY (this means the same thing as あと, but sounds more like you're 50 years old. It's not formal but a bit old fashioned and / or literary)

最後(さいご)

last ★★★★★

後輩(こうはい)

someone younger than you in school ★★☆☆☆
後 (afterwards / behind) + 輩 (older or younger colleague) = 後輩 (someone younger than you in school)
