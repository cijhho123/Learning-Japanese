上司(じょうし)

upper person ★★☆☆☆
上 (above) + 司 (administer) = 上司 (upper person)

anyone at work who out-ranks you
司会者(しかいしゃ)

master of ceremonies ★☆☆☆☆
司 (administer) + 会 (big meeting) + 者 (professional) = 司会者 (master of ceremonies)

a master of ceremonies of a TV show or event. It's not used for 'sucker MCs' which is a darn shame.
