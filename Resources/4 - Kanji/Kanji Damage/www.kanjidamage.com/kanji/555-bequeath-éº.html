遺 bequeath

(motion) + 貴 (exalted)
★★☆☆☆
15 strokes
BETA

Onyomi

I

Mnemonic

I bequeath you my money - i.e. I move it to you in hopes you will exalt me after I'm dead.

Jukugo

遺産(いさん)

inheritance ★☆☆☆☆
遺 (bequeath) + 産 (childbirth, production of things) = 遺産 (inheritance)

inheritance (but, sadly, you can't say 'isan suru' to mean 'inherit.'- you have to say 遺産を相続する　（いさん　を　　そうぞく　する））. . . .fuck!
遺伝子(いでんし)

gene ☆☆☆☆☆
遺 (bequeath) + 伝 (transmit) + 子 (child) = 遺伝子 (gene)

a gene (heredity is 遺伝　（いでん))
