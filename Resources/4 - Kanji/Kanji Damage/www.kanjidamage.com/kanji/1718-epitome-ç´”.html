純 epitome

糸 (string) + 屯 (bent dagger)
★★★☆☆
10 strokes
BETA

Onyomi

JUN

The weather in JUNE is the epitome of spring.

Mnemonic

Using string to try and mend a bent dagger is the epitome of stinginess.

Jukugo

純粋(じゅんすい) な

essence ★★★☆☆
純 (epitome) + 粋 (essence) = 純粋 (essence)

the essence of something. Pure pleasure! Pure blood! Pure honor!
単純(たんじゅん) な

simple version ★☆☆☆☆
単 (merely) + 純 (epitome) = 単純 (simple version)

the simple kind of X as opposed to the complicated kind (not rude). Also, simple-minded, mouth-breathing person (pretty rude).
