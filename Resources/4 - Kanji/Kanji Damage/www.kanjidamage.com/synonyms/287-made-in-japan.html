   made in japan

日本製(にほんせい) Things (usually electronics or clothes) made in Japan.
 
日本出身(しゅっしん) A person WHO COMES FROM a certain place. “どちらの御出身？”＝ Which city were you born in? Depending on the circumstance, it can mean place of birth, which college you came from, which country . ..
 
日本方(かた) A person who COMES FROM a certain place. More formal than 出身. Beware: You can’t say “I’m America の方” nor can you say “I’m America の出身”. They’re used when asking someone else.
