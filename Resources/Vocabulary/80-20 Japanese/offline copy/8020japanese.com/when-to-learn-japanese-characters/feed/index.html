]]> By: Mike Druttman https://8020japanese.com/when-to-learn-japanese-characters/#comment-11463 Thu, 11 Nov 2021 06:20:26 +0000 https://8020japanese.com/?p=2802#comment-11463 This is all very useful and clear, Richard. I started with Romaji and then moved over to Hiragana and Katakana later on. Yes, it is a burden to learn these Japanese alphabets – but you truly CANNOT progress until they are embedded in your memory. Think of other languages with different alphabets like Russian or Greek. You could use a kind of ‘Romaji’ for them too. And yet, you cannot say that you’re starting to ‘crack’ the language until you can navigate around the original alphabets. I found that mastering H +K was ESSENTIAL along the way to saying that I’m really progressing with Japanese. Otherwise you are forever ‘on the outside, looking in’. My suggestion: don’t make Romaji a crutch for yourself.
